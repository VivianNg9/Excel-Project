<h1 align="center">Advanced Excel for Business – Coursera</h1> 

A practical, end-to-end Excel project focused on advanced spreadsheet design, complex formulas, data cleaning, financial functions, automated lookups, and professional dashboard building for business decision-making.

---

## Project Overview

This repository showcases advanced Microsoft Excel skills developed through the **Coursera – Advanced Excel for Business** course.  
The project emphasizes building **robust, auditable, and scalable spreadsheets** that support data-driven decisions in business, finance, and analytics contexts.

The focus goes beyond formulas to include **model structure, automation, clarity, and usability**, reflecting real-world expectations of business and financial analysts.

---
